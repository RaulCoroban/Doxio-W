Need a break or oil change? Or maybe a in-depth inspection? Tell FlagUp what you need and it will solve it for you!

By posting a request on the website with a few details about your vehicle, FlagUp is able to find you the best choice, either filtered by price or by distance. Ok, but how can I do this?

The Web 🌐

A universal web platform to access the tool. First, one must enter the website and fill in the form, which consists of 6-8 steps. A simple interface is provided for each so it only asks for a specific field.

On the Repair Shop side, requests are received so they can offer an accurate quote to the client while watching what competitors have to say about the same job.

The App 📱

Also, FlagUp can be accessed from an iOS platform. The interface offers again uncluttered information, keeping it all simple.

The Promo 🚩

As this is a brave entrepreneur project, some promotion is needed in order to attract investors attention. This is why we have a promo material for pitches (right) with full information about the benefits of joining the tool, and also collaboration flyers (left).

And if the team has convinced you, we have to make sure you can contact us. Classic tactic. Business cards (magnifying glass).

All icons have been designed specifically for this app.
